Dharwad – A City of Music

Dharwad and its surrounding region have produced and inspired many celebrated classical musicians. The city remains a living centre of Hindustani classical music and cultural learning.

Notable Musicians Associated with Dharwad

Kumar Gandharva

Kumar Gandharva (1924–1992) was a famous Hindustani classical singer from near Dharwad. Known for his unique voice and creative style, he introduced new ragas and blended folk music with classical traditions. Despite illness, he continued singing with great passion and received the Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan awards for his contribution to Indian music..

Mallikarjun Mansur

Mallikarjun Mansur (1910–1992) was a legendary Hindustani classical singer from Dharwad. He belonged to the Jaipur-Atrauli Gharana and was known for his powerful voice and mastery of rare ragas. His singing was full of emotion and depth, making him one of India’s greatest vocalists. He received many awards, including the Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan, for his outstanding contribution to Indian classical music..

Gangubai Hangal

Gangubai Hangal (1913–2009) was a renowned Hindustani classical vocalist from Dharwad. She belonged to the Kirana Gharana and was known for her deep, powerful voice and expressive singing. Over her long career, she became one of India’s most respected musicians and received top honors, including the Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan.

Sawai Gandharva

Sawai Gandharva (1886–1952) was a famous Hindustani classical singer from Dharwad. He was a disciple of the great Ustad Abdul Karim Khan and became a leading artist of the Kirana Gharana. Known for his rich voice and graceful singing, he trained many famous students, including Bhimsen Joshi and Gangubai Hangal. The Sawai Gandharva Music Festival held in Pune every year is named in his honor..

Music Education & Institutions

Dharwad’s colleges and cultural trusts support music education through formal courses and practical training. Local gurukuls and music schools continue the oral tradition of teaching.
