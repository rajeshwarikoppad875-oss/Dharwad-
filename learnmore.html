Dharwad District

Dharwad district has 5 taluks: Dharwad, Hubli, Kalghatgi, Navalgund, and Kundgol. These are the administrative sub-divisions of the district for local governance and revenue collection.

Explore Dharwad

Kundgol

Kundgol is a town in the Dharwad district of Karnataka, and the total number of villages in the Kundgol taluk is 159. It is located about 14 km south-west of Hubli.

Kalagatagi

Kalagatagi is an administrative taluka in the Dharwad district of Karnataka, which contains a total of 87 villages. It is one of five talukas in the Dharwad district.

Navalgund

Navalgund is a town and taluk in the Dharwad district of Karnataka, and it has a total of 108 villages. The town of Navalgund is a hub for the famous hand-woven carpets known as 'Navalgund Jamakhan'.

Hubli

Hubli is the official name of Hubballi, an urban center in Karnataka's Dharwad District, while Hubli taluk is a rural administrative division within the district. The Hubli taluk is home to a total of 58 villages.

Dharwad

Dharwad is a city and district headquarters in Karnataka's northwestern part, forming a twin city urban area with Hubballi. The Dharwad taluk has a total of 118 villages.
