Dharwad is a prominent educational hub in Karnataka, earning it the nickname "Vidya Kashi". It hosts numerous institutions, including the renowned Karnatak University, the University of Agricultural Sciences, and colleges like the SDM College of Engineering and Technology and Sri Chaitanya Techno School. The city attracts students from across India and abroad due to its concentration of quality educational facilities, which include a wide range of colleges, universities, and professional institutions.

Leading Educational Institutions

üéì Karnatak University, Dharwad (KUD)

Karnatak University, established in 1949, is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in Karnataka. It offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs in arts, science, commerce, and management. The beautiful campus in Pavate Nagar promotes a peaceful learning environment. KUD is known for its dedicated faculty, strong academic heritage, and community engagement. It has been a key center for higher education and research in North Karnataka for decades.

IIT Dharwad

The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Dharwad is a premier public engineering and research institute established in 2016 by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. It is a third-generation IIT and was mentored by IIT Bombay to ensure academic rigour and quality.

SDM College of Engineering

Shri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwara College of Engineering & Technology, Dharwad was established in the year 1979 under the flagship of SDME Society and has grown into one of the nationally acclaimed premier engineering institutions in the country. The Institution has been consistently rated high, ranking within 50, among the top private engineering colleges in the country.

KIMS Hubli

Karnataka Institute of Medical Sciences (KIMS) Hubli is a government medical college and tertiary care hospital in North Karnataka. It offers undergraduate (MBBS) and postgraduate (MD/MS), and super-specialty (DM/MCh) degrees, affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS). KIMS provides free healthcare to poor patients and serves over 12 neighboring districts with state-of-the-art facilities and research opportunities.

BVB Hubli

BVB College Hubli, officially known as KLE Technological University (KLETU), is a private university founded in 1947 in Hubballi, Karnataka. It is known for its engineering and technology programs, with a campus spread over 52 acres and approved by NAAC, AICTE, and NBA. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral courses and has a strong placement record.

JSS College of Arts, Commerce and Science, located in Dharwad, is a renowned institution under the JSS Mahavidyapeetha. It offers quality education in arts, commerce, and science streams with well-equipped laboratories and experienced faculty. The college emphasizes both academic excellence and cultural development among students. With various extracurricular activities and student support programs, it encourages all-round growth. JSS College continues to play a vital role in shaping the educational landscape of Dharwad.
